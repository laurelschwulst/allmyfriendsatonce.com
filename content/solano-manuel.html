I was 13

I greeted the chat room in my usual manner:

“I'm 13. Would anyone like to fuck me?”

Private messages that came immediately.

“I'm 34. I would fuck you”, you said.

“hi”

“hi. are you really 13?”

“yes”

“nice. have you ever done it before?”

“no”

“what would you like to do?”

“I dunno. describe what you would do to me if we were in the same room now”

It makes me slightly uncomfortable with myself to think that we didn't meet until I was 18 and then, you were the one I lost my virginity to. Well, some of it.

It makes me feel weird because it makes me question myself. Why didn't I ever meet someone else in those teenage years in between? Someone I knew in real life. Someone my age.

And then when we finally met, I kept asking myself: “what was that for??”

I'm 26 now. I hadn't had anything to do with you in at least 6 years.

Until I found out online, last week, that you'd died.
